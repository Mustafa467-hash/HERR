Poetries i have wrote for her
(more will be added)

Dil dhoondta hai phir wahi fursat ke raat din,
Bas unki baat ho, hum sunte chalein haseen.
Woh rang-e-zindagi ka naye silsile bana,
Jo lafz-e-ishq unki muskurahat se saja.
Har saans mein basi ho unki khushboo ka nasha,
Ishq ki narmi se khilta rahe yeh dastaan, Bus yehi hai dua.

Ishq par zor nahin, hai yeh woh aag Ghalib,
Jo lagaye na lage, aur bujhaye na bujhe.
Dil se guzarta hai, ek narm hawa ki tarah,
Uski khushboo mein dil ka aashiyaan basa hai.
Yeh chaandni raat, aur unka saath, kya baat,
Yeh silsila rahe sada, bas yahi hai dua.

Mohabbat thi unse, is kadar ke yaad aaya ab bhi,
Khuda ne poocha kyun ki, humne kaha uske khaatir.
Yeh ishq hai, ya ek ibadat ki shama jalti,
Jismein roshan hai unka chehra, mere dil ki qaseer.
Har saans mein unka zikr, har dhadkan mein unka asar,
Yeh silsila-e-ishq, yeh hai dard-e-jigar.

Ishq ki aag se roshan mere khayalon ki duniya,
Jisne kiya isse mehsoos, wo samjha jahan ko.
Ek pyaas hai jo kabhi bujhti nahin, kabhi mit'ti nahin,
Usi pyaas ka hai sukoon, usi khushi ka hai jahan.
Jo ishq mein hai gum, wo paaye khuda ka raasta,
Yeh mehsoos karne ki cheez, bayaan kaise karein.

Tere ishq ki inteha chahta hoon,
Meri saari wafa, teri nazar chahta hoon.
Yeh raat, yeh tanhai, yeh chand ka nasha,
Tere saath har lamha, bas khudaa chahta hoon.
Yeh meri zindagi ki kitab, tere naam ho,
Is dastaan ko bas tere pyar ka paighaam ho.

Kehte hain jise ishq, khudaya, kya cheez hai,
Ik baar muskura do, saari duniya fida hai.
Woh nigahein jo chhupi hain, usi mein hai asar,
Us ek nazar mein hai saari zindagi ki khushi.
Ishq mein tere, jo hai halchal, woh hai ajab,
Yeh dil jo hai usi ka, yeh saans usi ki hai.

Chandni raaton mein teri baatein yaad aati hain,
Dil ki har dhadkan mein teri hi sada aati hai.
Teri aankhon mein jo khoye, raaste mil gaye,
Pyar ki manzil pe hum yun hi chalte chale aate hain.
Phoolon ki tarah tu muskuraye har pal,
Tere ishq mein yeh zindagi gulzaar ho jati hai.

Dil se dil tak, ik raah bani hai,
Ishq ki khushboo se saji kahani hai.
Tere bin, jeene ka kya maja,
Teri nigaahon mein meri zindagi hai.
Aaj bhi teri yaadon ki baarish hai,
Har lamha, har ghadi, bas tujhse pyaar hai.
